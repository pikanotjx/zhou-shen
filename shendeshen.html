深的深 is Zhou Shen's debut album released on 6th November 2017.

The album was produced by Yin Yue, Gao Xiao Song, Qian Lei

In multiple interviews, Zhou Shen has credited his success in the music industry to Gao Xiao Song. This is because after getting disqualified in the second round of The Voice of China, Zhou Shen had no intentions of pursuing a singing career. However, it was Gao Xiao Song who reached how to him and made him go after his dreams after seeing his talent on the very same show.
