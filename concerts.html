TME "Goodnight, See You Tomorrow" Online Concert

25th July 2020

Goodnight, See You Tomorrow is an online concert by Tencent Music Entertainment.

It was held on 25th July 2020 and featured some of Zhou Shen's well known songs including 大鱼 (Big Fish) and 影 (Shadow). Live broadcasts were available through QQ Music, Kugou Music, Kuwo Music, Tencent WeSing, and Tencent WeSee. The concert video was later was released on YouTube.

Planet C-929 Concert Tour

9th November 2019 - 4th January 2020

Planet C-929 was announced on 12th September 2019. C-929 was inspired by Zhou Shen's english name, Charlie as well as his birthday which is the 9th of September. The planet was inspired by a fairytale that Zhou Shen has always liked, "The Little Prince".

At the Guangzhou concert, it was annouced that 5 more concerts will be held in Wuhan, Guiyang, Qingdao, Hefei and Beijing. However, these concerts could not happen due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

Concert Locations

  • 09 November 2019, Beijing
  • 16 November 2019, Nanjing
  • 30 November 2019, Shenzhen
  • 07 December 2019, Suzhou
  • 14 December 2019, Chengdu
  • 21 December 2019, Shanghai
  • 04 January 2020, Guangzhou

深空间

May 2018 - 2019

深空间 (literally 'Deep Space') is Zhou Shen's first ever concert. It started in May 2018, and was held throughout China.

As part of the concert, there was also a Nanyang Khek Community Gift of Warm Charity Concert that was held in Singapore in 2019. This was also Zhou Shen's first time in Singapore.
