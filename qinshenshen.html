勤深深 is a singer duo with 勤 representing Hacken Lee (李克勤) and 深深 representing Zhou Shen.

The group was formed as part of the show Our Song in 2019.

Through the show, the duo sang covers of multiple songs including 追 (Chase), 天下有情人 (Lovers Under the Sky)

and some other significant pieces including Hacken Lee's songs.

At the end of the show, thanks to their overwhelming popularity, the duo was crowned the Golden Duo. They also returned in the next season to perform 2 more songs.

  1. 突如其来的爱 (Sudden Love)
  2. 富士山下 (Below Mount Fuji)
  3. 天下有亲人 (Lovers Under the Sky)
  4. 那片海 (That Sea)
  5. 月半小夜曲
  6. 追 (Chase)
  7. 大会堂给演奏厅
  8. 画 (Drawing)
  9. 心如刀割
  10. 野狼 Disco (A Wisp of Fascination)
  11. 不见就散
  12. 你的名字我的姓氏
  13. 希望 (Hope)
